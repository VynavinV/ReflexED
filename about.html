About ReflexED

Our Mission

ReflexED bridges the gap between teachers and diverse learners by transforming a single lesson upload into multiple inclusive, personalized learning experiences. We believe every student deserves content that matches their unique learning style, language, and accessibility needs.

How It Works

1

Teachers Upload

Teachers upload their lesson materials once — any format, any subject.

2

AI Transforms

Our AI analyzes content and generates personalized versions for different learning styles.

3

Students Learn

Each student receives content tailored to their profile — visual, audio, simplified, or gamified.

Subject-Specific AI Modes

🗣️ Language Arts (Foreign Language)

AI Translation Coach

Like Google Translate, but instead of direct answers, AI asks guided questions about tense, vocabulary, grammar, and cultural context to build critical thinking skills.

🔬 Math & Science

Manim Mode

Visual step-by-step animations and diagram-based explanations.

📜 History

Story Mode

Timeline-based narrated stories with historical context.

🌍 Geography

GeoGames

Map-based games and interactive geographical activities.
